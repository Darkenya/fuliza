Your Privacy Matters to Us

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Fuliza Boost services, we collect the following information:

  • Personal identification information (ID Number)
  • Contact information (M-Pesa phone number)
  • Transaction details related to your limit increase request
  • Device information and usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your Fuliza limit increase requests
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Communicate with you about your transactions
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure data storage with regular backups
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only with:

  • Payment processors to complete transactions
  • Law enforcement when legally required
  • Service providers who assist our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • File a complaint with relevant authorities

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze usage patterns. You can control cookie settings through your browser.
